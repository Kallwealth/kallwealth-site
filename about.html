What we are about

Our Target

At Kallwealth our vision statement is to be a reputable and competent indigenous facility engineering company with a global competitiveness and strength.

We aim to improve on and maintain a high standard of engineering practice, in environmental, construction, procurement, facility and project management services.

Our Background

Kallwealth was established in June 2002 and has grown into an engineering success story. We have an authorized share capital of 10 million Naira and a working capital of over 100 million Naira.

We've worked with numerous companies for the past 18 years, and successfully completed all projects, all testimonies from our clients have been positive therefore improving our public image as a high quality service delivering company.
